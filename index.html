Features

Designed to disappear.

The best technology is the kind you don't think about.

⚡

Always-On, Zero Friction

No app to launch, no button to tap. FaceTag runs passively in the background from the moment you put on your glasses.

🎤

Automatic Capture

When someone introduces themselves, the glasses listen and store the details. Their name, role, fun fact — captured from natural conversation.

🔒

Privacy-First Architecture

All face embeddings and data stay on your device. Nothing is uploaded. Nothing is shared. Ever.

🪶

49g All-Day Wearable

Rokid Glasses weigh less than most sunglasses. Wear them all day without fatigue or self-consciousness.

Privacy

Your data never leaves your device.

🛡️
  • ✓ No cloud storage — everything runs on-device via Qualcomm AR1
  • ✓ No photos stored — only mathematical face embeddings
  • ✓ No server communication — zero network calls for recognition
  • ✓ Auto-expiry for unlabeled faces — unsaved data disappears
  • ✓ One-tap delete all — instantly wipe everything, no questions asked
  • ✓ Audio processed in real-time — conversations are never recorded or stored
